Fifth Generation (Present and Beyond)

The fifth generation computers are based on the Artificial Intelligence (AI) technique. Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science concerned with making computers that can behave like humans. Computers having AI are still in the process of development. The developers are trying to create machines that can interpret and respond to nature language and can make decisions in a real-life situation as humans can do. Some technologies are bieng used today; for example; voice recognition.
In the area of robotics; computers are also widely used for a variety of tasks like space exploration;building cars and so on. The AIBO made by Sony features a variety of sences such as touch; hearing; sight and balance.
