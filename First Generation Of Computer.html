First Generation Computer (1945-1956)

The first generation computer used vaccum tubes as the main electronic component. ENIAC;EDVAC and UNIVAC 1 are example of first generation computer.

Advantages
These computers were the fastest calculating device of their time. The ENIAC was capable of performing 5000 additions or 350 multiplication per second.
Disadvantages
They were huge in size and heavy in wieght.
They emitted a large amount of heat because of the usage of thousands of vaccum tubes.
They consumed more power and were very expensive.
They used machine language which was cumbersome and machine-dependent.That is a program written for one computer could not be executed on another computer.
